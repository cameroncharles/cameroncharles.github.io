View in VR

Monument to Lost Villagesclose

A monument to all the villages that were razed by the liquidators shortly after the disaster in an effort to reduce the radiation levels.

Each candle holder on the cement representation of the exclusion zone represents one of the 97 villages lost.

Wikipedia: Chornobyl Disaster

Duga radarclose

Large Soviet radar system that formed part of the Early Warning System that was, ironically, listening for nuclear threats from external sources.

Taken from the nearby memorial for the heroic workers who lost their lives trying to extinguish the highly radioactive flames immediately after the disaster.
